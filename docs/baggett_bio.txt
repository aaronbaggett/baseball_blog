Aaron Baggett lives in Belton, Texas.
Aaron is currently an Assistant Professor of Psychology at the University of Mary Hardin-Baylor.  He teaches courses in introductory statistics and experimental design for the social sciences.  A former high school and college baseball umpire, Aaron is primarily interested in modeling MLB umpire ball—strike judgment and decision making patterns using PITCHf/x data.
